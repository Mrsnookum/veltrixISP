Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect the data of our ISP partners and their Wi-Fi network end-users.
Effective Date: March 2026

1. Introduction & Roles

Veltrix provides a cloud-based billing and radius management platform. In the context of data privacy laws:

  • Veltrix is the Data Processor: We process data on behalf of our ISP clients to facilitate network access and billing.
  • The ISP Owner is the Data Controller: The individual or company running the local Wi-Fi network is responsible for obtaining consent from their end-users.

2. Information We Collect

A. From ISP Owners (Our Direct Clients):

  • Account Data: Business name, subdomain preferences, admin email addresses, and encrypted dashboard passwords.
  • Hardware & API Credentials: MikroTik Router IP addresses, API usernames, and passwords (required to push user expiry commands to your router).
  • Payment Gateway Keys: PayHero Channel IDs and Basic Auth Tokens to route M-Pesa payments directly to your till/paybill.

B. From End-Users (Wi-Fi Customers):

  • Device Identifiers: MAC Addresses and IP Addresses to authenticate sessions on the local network.
  • Billing Information: Phone numbers entered on the captive portal to trigger M-Pesa STK pushes.
  • Transaction Records: M-Pesa receipt references, timestamps, and purchased package details. We do NOT store M-Pesa PINs.

3. How We Use the Data

We collect and process this data strictly to provide the Veltrix software service:

  • To authenticate devices on the MikroTik router and grant internet access.
  • To trigger automated payment requests (STK Pushes) via third-party gateways.
  • To generate financial dashboards, revenue analytics, and transaction logs for the ISP Owner.
  • To detect, prevent, and troubleshoot technical issues or fraudulent access attempts.

4. Third-Party Data Sharing

Veltrix does not sell or rent personal data to marketers or data brokers. We only share information with trusted third parties necessary to operate the platform:

  • Payment Processors: Phone numbers and transaction amounts are securely transmitted to PayHero (or your configured custom gateway) to process M-Pesa payments.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Our databases and web applications are securely hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure (Google Workspace / Apps Script).

5. Data Security & Retention

We implement strict security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data.

  • All communications between the Captive Portal, the Veltrix Cloud API, and Payment Gateways are encrypted via HTTPS/SSL.
  • End-user transaction data is retained as long as the ISP Owner's account is active, to provide historical revenue analytics.
  • If an ISP Owner terminates their Veltrix subscription, their dedicated database instance and all associated end-user data are permanently scheduled for deletion within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (such as the Kenya Data Protection Act), you have rights regarding your personal data:

  • End-Users: Because Veltrix acts as a Data Processor, end-users seeking to access, correct, or delete their phone numbers or connection logs must contact their local ISP Provider directly. Veltrix will comply with deletion requests authorized by the ISP Owner.
  • ISP Owners: You may request a full export of your billing database or request complete account deletion by contacting Veltrix support.
