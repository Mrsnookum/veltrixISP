Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Veltrix billing automation and MikroTik synchronization.

M-Pesa payment confirmed but user is not redirected?

This usually happens if the M-Pesa callback URL is blocked by your firewall. Ensure your MikroTik allows incoming traffic from our cloud sync servers to trigger the session activation.

How does device locking (MAC binding) work?

Once a user pays, Veltrix captures the MAC address of the device used for payment. That voucher is then permanently bound to that device, preventing other users from using the same code.

Do I need a static IP for Veltrix to work?

No. Veltrix uses a specialized Cloud-Sync script that works even on dynamic IPs. Your router will periodically "check-in" with our central command to update user lists.

What happens if M-Pesa is down?

Veltrix includes a manual override feature for admins. You can generate a "Rescue Voucher" from your dashboard to get your clients online until the M-Pesa service is restored.
